# pokedex-js v1.2

## index.html

Here you will be able to find a collection of 151 Pokémon.

### Guide

1. Choose a Pokémon type of choice.
2. Click catch on any Pokémon you would like, you can only catch one of each Pokémon.
3. Click [here](./collection.html) to view your collection (yes this is currently the only way to view your collection).

## collection.html 

Here you can view your caught Pokémon.

### Guide

Click on "Release" to remove a Pokémon from your pokédex.
